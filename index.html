ðŸ“Š Performance Analysis

The simulator efficiently demonstrates disk head movement patterns and total seek time for each algorithm. Users can clearly see performance differences:

  • SSTF usually performs better than FCFS by selecting the nearest request, reducing overall seek time
  • LOOK/C-LOOK avoid unnecessary movement to disk ends, improving efficiency over SCAN/C-SCAN
  • SCAN/C-SCAN prevent starvation by ensuring all requests are eventually serviced

The project successfully meets educational objectives by connecting disk scheduling theory to visualization and practical execution.
